Alternately.learn

The Internet has great resources available for every topic for free, but it's jumbled, explained in parts and isn’t structured which can be overwhelming.

Alternately.learn is a curated set of resources for all the sub topics of a specific topic (AI/ML) with levels of difficulty (Beginner/ Intermediate/ Advanced) for each topic, which includes:

  • The best resources across the internet for a particular topic.

  • Why the resource is important.

  • Who created it.

  • Where it can be found.

  • The prerequisites and difficulty level of the resource and

  • An interactive dashboard where you can see the reviews, overall satisfaction score and a summary with a sentiment analysis taken across multiple platforms for every resource.

Alternately.build

Alternately is more than just learning from theory- Alternately.build directs you industry relevant projects which will help you solidify your understanding from topics from alternatively.learn

Doing this can help you in:

  • Help you understand industry level projects

  • Proof-Of-work during interviews.

  • Figuring out how to apply what you have learnt to real world applications.

Alternately.test

Once you’ve learnt the subject matter and have built out a few projects, it’s time to test your knowledge by taking a test.

This test will grade you on the different areas of AI/ML. Each test submission will be personally reviewed and suggestions will be made on where you are doing well and which topics you will need to revisit and improve your understanding.

How it works:

  • You take the test. You’re given a set of 30 questions from different areas of AI/ML.

  • Once you submit the test, it will be reviewed by us and you will get personalised suggestions on where you have to improve and links to content which can help you improve in the topics where you are lagging.

Alternately.career

Alternately.career helps you to put yourself out there to get a job/internship in the field of AI/ ML. Alternately.career is divided in two parts, prepare and apply

Prepare includes:

  • Draft and design the perfect CV/Resume to chart out best opportunities

  • Optimize your LinkedIn profile to make it appealing to employers

  • Build your own portfolio websites and add your case studies and projects to it and much more!

  • Reach out to people using cold emails and get opportunities in startups etc

  • How to prepare for an interview and crack interviews at top companies.

Apply includes:

  • Buy well designed, research backed resume templates which you can edit to your own liking within seconds.

  • Get portfolio templates and designs which you can use as a building block for your website.

  • Case study examples with structure which you can use to emulate your own case studies on and a whole lot more!

Alternately.connect

Alternately.connect helps you connect with people who are on the same journey as you are on as well as people with experience who can help answer your queries and doubts.

This includes:

  • A discord community where learners can discuss and get answers to their doubts and talk about everything ML/AI.

  • A question board where anyone can ask a question and get answers to their questions within 24 hours. The top questions will be pinned to a board which future learners can access.

  • Bi-weekly calls within the community of learners to discuss new topics and updates in the world of AI/ML.

About me

Hi! My name is Kevin. I graduated in Mechanical Engineering from Loyola in Chennai. I wasn't very interested in pursuing a career in Engineering so I took a year off to study for CAT in the hope of getting into IIM's.


The only reason I started preparing for CAT is that I didn't know of all the other career paths available like freelancing, starting a web design agency etc. I got 95 percentile in CAT and was called for interviews to many IIM's but my interest lay elsewhere.


So I started my own small e-commerce brand and now help other medium to large sized Foreign as well as Indian e-commerce brands in their marketing and advertising.


But I realised that it was because of my year off that I was able to explore other fields like advertising, marketing and design. But many do not have the opportunity to take a year off.


And I got to know that there's so many ways in which you can make a rewarding career and not be stuck in a job you hate. But even when I tried to learn stuff on my own, I found it mostly catered to the American and European audience.


So I decided to start Alernately to help Indian college students and graduates learn about the other career and business opportunities available in the market along with topics such as how to handle finances, taxes, the stock market, receiving payments from clients, invoicing and much more.

Helping college students and graduates learn and implement rewarding,
alternative career choices by making it simple for them to build businesses
and startups.
